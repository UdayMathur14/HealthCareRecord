What is EHR?

Electronic Health Records(EHR) are widely used in many developed nations of the world providing efficiency & saving time for doctors

Why EHR?

The goal of an EHR is to organize patient data, creating it simpler for healthcare staff to evaluate a patient's record before or while their visit. With the old method of physical file folders, you could simply have health records scattered across various doctor's offices

Benefits of EHR

EHR can improve patient care by:
  1. Reducing medical errors that may incur
  2. Availability of health information 24x7
  3. Avoiding delays and duplication of tests
  4. Improve doctor's ability to provide better care
  5. Expose potential safety hazards or warn against adverse events before they happen
  6. Produce clearer records of what occurred during treatment
